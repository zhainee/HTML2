Omelette Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 5 tablespoons Oil divided or as needed
  • 2 medium Potatoes cubed
  • 1 head Garlic minced
  • 1 370-ml can corned beef
  • 1 large white onion
  • Salt to taste

Instruction:

  1. In a large pan over medium heat, preheat 3 tablespoons oil. Fry the potatoes. Set aside when cooked.
  2. Add 2 tablespoons oil to the frying pan. Add garlic and saut√© until lightly browned.
  3. Add corned beef then return the cooked potatoes to the pan. Let it come to a simmer.
  4. Add onions and stir. Cook depending on how dry you want your corned beef. Season to taste with salt. Serve hot with rice.
